# Code of Conduct

Hermes WeChat Bridge is committed to a welcoming, respectful, and productive community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while staying precise about technical risks.
- Prefer reproducible reports, clear evidence, and actionable suggestions.
- Respect privacy. Do not post real tokens, private chat content, customer data, or personal identifiers.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private credentials, logs, screenshots, or message contents without permission.
- Repeatedly derailing issues or pull requests after maintainers ask to refocus.
- Using the project to abuse WeChat, Hermes, users, or third-party services.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove content; close issues; block users; or escalate security-sensitive reports to private channels. Enforcement should be proportional, documented when appropriate, and focused on community safety.

## Reporting

For community conduct concerns, contact the maintainers privately. For security issues, follow `SECURITY.md`.
